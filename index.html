How to play

Nictionary

Guess one hidden five-letter word in six tries.

  1. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word.
  2. Use the tile colors to narrow down the answer.
  3. Daily puzzles build streaks; Practice has separate statistics.

Brain Melt

One guess is applied to four different hidden words at the same time.

  1. Each board evaluates the same guess independently.
  2. A solved board stops adding new guess rows.
  3. Solve as many of the four boards as possible within six guesses.

Absurdle

Absurdle avoids committing to one answer until your guesses force it to.

  1. After each guess, it keeps the largest remaining group of possible answers.
  2. The header shows how many possible answers remain.
  3. You have unlimited guesses; the board scrolls as it grows.

How to play

Guess the hidden five-letter word in six tries. Each guess must be a valid word. After each guess, the tiles show how close you are.

Daily gives everyone one fixed puzzle per calendar day. Practice gives you unlimited random puzzles.

Solutions come from a carefully curated pool of familiar words. The accepted guess list is intentionally broader.

Nictionary is an independent, locally stored game. It is not affiliated with Wordle or The New York Times.
